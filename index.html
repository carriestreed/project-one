Rules of the Race

Your objective is to be the first to exit the opposite side of the maze. Hit the appropriate keys to move your marker, but do not hold the keys down continuously. That would be considered cheating, and nobody likes cheaters. And also because I haven't written the code to make that happen for both players at the same time yet, so yea.. don't do it.

Player One Controls
D key moves right
A key moves left
W key moves up
S key moves down


Player Two Controls
Right Arrow moves right
Left Arrow moves left
Up Arrow moves up
Down Arrow moves down
